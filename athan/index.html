FAQ

Common questions

Athan books prayer times into your device's built-in calendar. To see and schedule around your Exchange or Outlook events, add that account to your device's calendar app:

On macOS: Open System Settings → Internet Accounts → Add Account → Microsoft Exchange. Sign in with your work credentials. Make sure Calendar is enabled. Your Exchange events will appear in Apple Calendar, and Athan will automatically see them when scheduling prayers.

On iOS: Go to Settings → Calendar → Accounts → Add Account → Microsoft Exchange. Sign in and enable Calendar sync. Athan will see your Exchange events alongside your personal calendar.

On Android: Add your Exchange or Outlook account in your device's calendar app. Once synced, Athan will be able to read your work events and schedule prayers around them.

Features

Lives in your menu bar, always one glance away. Choose to see the next prayer, current prayer, countdown, or just the icon.

What makes Athan different

Never miss a prayer, even on your busiest days

Life happens. Meetings shift, days get packed, and prayer times can slip through the cracks. Athan's booking wizard reserves prayer slots as early as possible within each prayer window, working around your existing commitments — weeks or even months ahead of time.

  • ✓ Auto-schedules prayers at the earliest available time in each prayer window
  • ✓ Reads your calendar to avoid conflicts with existing commitments
  • ✓ Plan ahead — book prayer times weeks or months in advance
  • ✓ Drag-and-drop to manually adjust any proposed slot
  • ✓ Books directly into your macOS Calendar with one click
  • ✓ Extended Jumu'ah duration with custom start times for Fridays
